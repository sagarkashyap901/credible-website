Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026. The short version: we collect the minimum, we sell nothing, and you can delete everything.

What we collect

  • Account data: if you create an account — your name, email address and an encrypted password. Nothing else.
  • Usage data: anonymous, aggregate page-view statistics so we know which stories are read. No cross-site tracking, no advertising identifiers.

How it's stored

Account data is stored in a managed PostgreSQL database (Supabase) hosted on AWS infrastructure in the Asia-Pacific (Mumbai) region. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt and are never readable by anyone, including us. All traffic is encrypted in transit over HTTPS/TLS.

What we never do

  • Sell, rent or share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers.
  • Run third-party advertising trackers on our pages.
  • Email you anything you didn't sign up for.

Your rights

You can request a copy of your data or full deletion of your account at any time by writing to sagarbusiness90@gmail.com. Deletion is permanent and completed within 7 days.
