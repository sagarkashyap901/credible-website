Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026. The short version: no refunds on payments already made, but you can cancel anytime to stop future billing.

No refunds

Credible membership is ₹119/month, billed on a recurring basis via Razorpay. Because membership grants immediate access to digital content, payments already made — including the current billing period — are non-refundable. This applies whether you've read one article or none.

Cancel anytime

You can cancel your membership at any time from your account page — one button, no fees, no questions asked. When you cancel:

  • You keep full access until the end of your current billing period.
  • You will not be charged again after that period ends.
  • No partial or pro-rated refund is issued for the unused portion of the current period.

Failed or duplicate payments

This policy covers change-of-mind cancellations, not billing errors. If you're charged twice for the same period, charged after already cancelling, or a payment fails on your end but money leaves your account, that's a mistake — email sagarbusiness90@gmail.com with your payment ID and we'll investigate and correct it. Legitimate billing errors are fixed regardless of the no-refund policy above.

How refunds are processed

Where a refund is issued under the billing-errors clause above, it is processed through Razorpay back to your original payment method. Razorpay does not charge for standard refunds, though transaction fees already collected are not reversed. Processing typically takes 5–7 business days to reflect in your account, depending on your bank.

Questions

Reach us at sagarbusiness90@gmail.com for anything related to billing, cancellations or this policy.
